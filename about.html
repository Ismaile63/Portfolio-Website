A short bio

Hello there! I am a web-developer, building, maintaining and managing websites is what I do! I enjoy creating and maintaining projects of all types, the more complex and nuance, the more interesting and fun it becomes. My experience in web-design started in college where I made my first website, since then, I have been coding on and off until now. Roughly a year ago I got back into programming in full swing, and have no intention of slowing down anytime soon!

I have a strong work ethic rooted in continuous, auto-didactic learning and development, for this reason, I have always sought out elaborate and convoluted subjects through which I can grow and learn, as a result of this learning, I have found my way back into web-development and am looking to continue my learning through a host of differing languages and concepts to expand both my knowledge and project portfolio.

I'm open to any job opportunities where I can contribute, learn and grow. If you have an opportunity that you feel matches my skills, do not hesitate to contact me.

Feel free to dive into some of my work on my projects page.
